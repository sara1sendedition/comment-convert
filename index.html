Common questions

Everything you'd want to ask before connecting an account.

Will Comment Convert post replies for me automatically?

No. Comment Convert generates draft replies for you to review. Nothing is published unless you explicitly take action — and even then, it's only on platforms whose APIs support that, with your prior consent.

What data does Comment Convert access?

The minimum needed to do its job: your basic account info from your platform login, and the comments on content you own or have authorized us to read. We don't access your DMs, video drafts, or analytics that aren't required for the inbox to function. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.

How does the AI learn my voice?

By looking at example replies you mark as "yes, that sounds like me." Drafts improve as you use the product. We don't train on your data to build models for other customers.

Can I disconnect my account at any time?

Yes. You can revoke access from inside Comment Convert or from your platform's connected‑apps settings. When you do, we delete the access tokens immediately and your stored comment data on the schedule outlined in our Privacy Policy.

Which platforms are supported?

YouTube is live in early access. TikTok is in active development — we're working through TikTok's app review process now, which is why you're reading this page.

How much does it cost?

Early access is free while we get the experience right. We'll share pricing well before any plan goes paid, and early access users get founder pricing.
